# Encryptorium

Independent applied cryptography research platform covering the post-quantum transition.

We produce original research, technical analysis, and open resources on PQC migration, post-quantum zero-knowledge proof systems, and cryptographic inventory tooling.

## What we publish

- **Research and analysis** on post-quantum cryptography standards, adoption, and migration challenges
- **PQ-ZK security analysis** of deployed zero-knowledge proof systems under quantum threat models
- **Open resources** including the [Post-Quantum Readiness Assessment](https://encryptorium.com/pq-readiness-assessment/) (PQRA) methodology
- **PQC timeline** tracking NIST, IETF, and regulatory milestones
- **Video** on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@encryptorium) — faceless video essays on the post-quantum transition
